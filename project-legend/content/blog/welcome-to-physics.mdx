# Welcome to Physics

Physics is the fundamental science that seeks to understand how the universe works—from the smallest particles to the largest galaxies. The goal of this blog is to share insights on teaching physics, making it accessible, and building conceptual clarity.

## Why Conceptual Understanding Matters

Memorizing formulas might help you pass an exam, but it won't help you truly understand physics. When you understand the *why* behind a formula, you can derive it, adapt it, and apply it to new situations. That's the difference between rote learning and real education.
